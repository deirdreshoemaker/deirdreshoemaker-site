About Me

I am a physicist specializing in numerical relativity and gravitational wave astrophysics. My work focuses on simulating black hole mergers and contributing to gravitational wave observatories like LIGO and Virgo. I am passionate about exploring the universe through Einstein's theory of gravity and mentoring the next generation of physicists.

Research

My research lies at the intersection of general relativity, astrophysics, and high-performance computing. I lead projects on black hole dynamics, waveform modeling, and gravitational wave data analysis. I collaborate with global teams to push the boundaries of what we know about the cosmos.
